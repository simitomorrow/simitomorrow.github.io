blubb.io

Description

blubb.io is a competitive puzzle game based on Puzzle Bobble.
In this game, colored bubbles are shot into a grid to align three matching colors next to each other, causing them to disappear.

blubb.io expands this concept by introducing a multiplayer mode where skillful dissolving of bubbles hinders the opponent's game instead of earning points.

My contributions to this project:

  • Everything about gameplay: Logic, inputhandling, visuals, balancing, stat tracking etc.
  • Realtime online multiplayer
  • Designed and built a physical arcademachine made of wood.
  • Collaborative UI and menu design with Zytronik
